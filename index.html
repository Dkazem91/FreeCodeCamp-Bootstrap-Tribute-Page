A Little Ol' Timeline

Date Event
November 7, 1867 A tiny kicking and screaming baby Marie is born unto this world, the world of Poland
1891 Skip a lot of boring child time and now she's ready to go France! Paris to be exact,and to study at Sorbonne to be exacter!
1895 Marie married Pierre Curie, which was when she transformed into the science beast that was forever to be known as Marie Curie
1897 Marie Curie spits out a kid, calls it Irene, turns into a mom
1902 Got hired for some experimenting and then guess what? Discoveres pure Radium. No big deal.
1903 Gets a Nobel Prize for their radioactivty. The Nobel-est of Prizes!
1904 Moving on up! got promoted to the CHIEF of the assistants to her husband in his lab
1906 Marie gets widowed! Husband dies tragic death! She replaces his teaching role and becomes the first female professor in Sorbonne!
1911 One Nobel Prize wasn't enough, this lady struck science gold once again, leveling up that radiactivity game
1914 The world says hey lets kill eachother for the first of two times, Marie Curie says not without my X-rays. Now wounded soldiers could see exactly what was killing them.
1922 Joins the French Academy of Medicine, uses her radiation powers for good.
1932 Specifically cancer. Joins forces with sister to opens up cancer fighting battle station, The Marie Sklodowska Curie Oncology Center.
1934 Marie Curie Dies from aplastic amenia at the tender age of 66, thought to have been brought on from years of being exposed to radiation

Marie Curie is one the most famous scientists, helped usher the world into the atomic age,paved roads for future female scientists, and was extremely modest and humble while doing so:

"Marie Curie is of all celebrated beings, the one whom fame has not corrupted"
Albert Einstein
